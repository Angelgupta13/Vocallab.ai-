# Competitor Analysis — Research Notes

## Competitive Landscape Matrix

### Global Players

#### Vapi
- **Funding:** $50M Series B
- **Self-serve:** Dashboard signup, instant API keys, 60 free minutes
- **Pricing:** $0.05/min platform fee + BYOK costs (pricing calculator on page)
- **Latency:** <500ms advertised
- **Compliance:** SOC2 + HIPAA + PCI
- **India:** No specific India page, but expanding via channel partners
- **Developer ecosystem:** 750K+ developers, extensive docs, Discord community

#### Retell AI
- **Pricing:** $0.07–$0.31/min bundled
- **Self-serve:** Dashboard signup, $10 free credit
- **Latency:** ~600ms advertised
- **Industry pages:** 9 dedicated pages (Healthcare, Financial Services, etc.)
- **Integrations:** Make, n8n, Zapier

#### Bland AI
- **Pricing:** $0.09/min flat all-in
- **Self-serve:** Dashboard signup, free tier
- **Languages:** 40+ including Hindi
- **Enterprise:** 250+ customers, 1.3B+ calls processed
- **Latency:** 400ms advertised

### India-Native Competitors

#### Gnani.ai
- **Funding:** $10M Series B (March 2026)
- **Stage:** Profitable, 200+ enterprise clients
- **Languages:** 12 Indian languages
- **Positioning:** "India's Leading Conversational AI Platform"
- **Go-to-market:** Enterprise sales (no self-serve) — but has case studies, language demos, and industry pages

#### Bolna
- **Funding:** $6.3M seed (General Catalyst + YC)
- **Growth:** 48% MoM revenue growth, 1050+ customers
- **Languages:** 10+ Indian languages
- **ARR:** Estimated $700K
- **Positioning:** "Voice AI for Indian enterprises"

## Key Insight: The Gnani.ai/Bolna Threat

Vapi, Retell, and Bland are global players who may enter India later. Gnani.ai and Bolna are *already* in Vocallabs' ICP today, with funding, Indian language support, and live deployments. For a bootstrapped company with zero funding, this is the more immediate existential threat — not Vapi.
