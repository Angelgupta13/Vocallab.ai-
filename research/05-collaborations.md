## Subspace Cross-Sell Thesis

The most overlooked collaboration is **Subspace itself**. Subspace manages subscriptions, group payments, and bill reminders — all use cases that benefit from voice calls. A user who misses a payment on a shared subscription gets an AI call: "Hi, this is Subspace. Your Netflix group contribution of ₹249 is overdue. Want to pay now?" Joey Dash owns both companies. Building a bridge between them costs integration effort and creates a defensible ecosystem.
